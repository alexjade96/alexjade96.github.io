Idea Tracker

I usually list potential far-future projects on a notepad (Sticky Notes on my PCs, Color Note on my phone, etc.) but it makes more sense to centralize them in one place instead. These ideas could or could not exist/be feasible, but they are interesting to think about.


Project ThemeDescriptionLikelihood?
Song/Playlist DownloaderUniversal playlist downloader from major music applications (e.g. Spotify, Youtube, etc.) Also allow for changing Song Metadata, maybe also mobile version for on-the-go mixtapes!Most likely to start work on next!
Room Planner2D/3D room universal room planner to help make it easier for furniture planning in a room. Ones I've seen/used all have some arbitrary quirks that make it difficult to use (e.g. actual brand-specific items instead of allowing for manual dimension changin)Long-term/currently unlikely
AI-powered resume job finderIt never made sense to me why people need to look for jobs instead of companies looking in some central database for people who would fit their needs.
